Neale Stadium, University of Idaho. Scoreboard. (1937)

This image is of the scoreboard from Neale Stadium, documenting the University of Idaho Vandas’ come from behind victory over the Oregon State College (now Oregon State University) Beavers, September 25, 1937. In the last 3 minutes of this exciting homecoming game, the Vandals scored a touchdown, winning 7 to 6. 

Construction began in 1936 for University of Idaho’s Athletic Stadium. The first game played here was the 1937 homecoming game against the O.S.C. Beavers, and it continued to be the venue for Idaho home games up through the 1968 football season. - Amy
